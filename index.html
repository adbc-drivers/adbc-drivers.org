What is adbc-drivers.org?

SELECT * FROM adbc_drivers;

ADBC is the modern data connectivity standard built to accelerate and simplify data access for analytics applications. And it's at an inflection point.

More projects and organizations are embracing ADBC and looking for drivers to connect to an ever-expanding landscape of data systems. But there's still much work to be done—both technical and social—to shape ADBC's next era.

That's why we're launching a new initiative: the ADBC Driver Foundry. If you're interested, please read our launch announcement to learn more.
